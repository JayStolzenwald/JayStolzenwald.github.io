Repertoire

Stage / Stand Up Show
30-60 min

The stage show is filled with humor, magic and joy. As many people from the audience get involved in the show, ZauberJanis transforms your event into amazing moments that you will surely remember for a long time. Whether it will be mentalism, virtuous performance or comedy: there always is something for everyone.


 

 

Close Up / At Table
30-90 min

''... I would have liked to see that from close up''
That is a common reaction after you just have seen a stage performance. ZauberJanis fulfills this wish by performing Close Up Show where the magic happens at the guest's table right under their watchful eyes. Sometimes the audience is even able to feel it in their own hands. You never experienced magic in such an intense and exiting way before.



Kids Magic
30-45 min

Whether it is a birthday party or other kids events: magic always ensures bright smiles, excitement and pure fun.
The participation of the kids forms the heart of the show. By the motto ''join the magic experience'' everyone gets involved in the show. Being part of a miracle is one of the best things a child could ever dream of.




Special Events

To have a suitable show, it can be adapted to you specific event like weddings, Christmas or Easter.
